Class Interviews

We offer three class interview time slots per week. To interview one of our staff members for a class paper or project, please click on the button above. Keep in mind that we may not be able to accommodate your request if the available staff member does not have expertise in the interview topic.

If we can't provide an interview for you, we may be able to provide you with the name(s) and phone number(s) of other mental health professionals in Austin who are willing to be interviewed by UT students. In addition, our website provides information on many areas relating to mental health which may provide you with the information you are seeking.
