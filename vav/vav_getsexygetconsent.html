Get Sexy, Get Consent ©

Get Sexy. Get Consent. © is a highly interactive one-hour program that examines how we negotiate sex and consent, boundaries and safety. Student actor-facilitators engage audiences through scenes, monologues, and conversations about consent. Get Sexy. Get Consent. is inclusive of all romantic and sexual relationships, is relevant for all genders and sexualities, and explores situations of abstinence, long-term relationships and one-night-stands.

This program addresses the following questions:
What is consent?
How do you know someone else wants what you want?
How do you communicate your boundaries?
Do you get it?

After attending a performance 75% of students reported they would act differently in future sexual situations based on what they learned in the performance.
