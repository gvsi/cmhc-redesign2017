Overcoming Anxiety with Yoga and Mindfulness

Tuesdays
10am - 12pm

All groups are free and confidential.

Does anxiety make it difficult for you to focus, quiet your mind, or just generally enjoy your life? Is your body constantly stressed, making it hard to sit still, sleep, or wind down when you want to relax? Perhaps you have tried to manage the anxiety with a variety of things, like staying really busy, or even alcohol/drugs. If you are looking to decrease anxiety by soothing your body and mind in healthy ways, consider joining this class.
