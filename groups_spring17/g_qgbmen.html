Queer/Gay/Bisexual Men's Group

Wednesdays
11am - Noon

All groups are free and confidential.

This group is a supportive place to discuss and work through a variety of issues with gay, bisexual, and queer identified men. Issues can include dating concerns, sexual concerns, difficulty with intimacy, questioning your own sexual identity, coping with family issues, coming out and living as an out person, difficulty fitting in in the LGBTQ community, and much more.

In most cases, before joining a group or class, students meet with the group leaders to determine if a particular group is a good fit for them. To set up a pre-group interview, please call (512) 471-3515.
