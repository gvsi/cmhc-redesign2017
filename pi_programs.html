Psychology Internship Program

The doctoral internship program in health service psychology at the Counseling and Mental Health Center at The University of Texas at Austin is now welcoming applications for the 2017-2018 training year. The application deadline is November 2, 2016, and we welcome and encourage applications from individuals from diverse backgrounds and life experiences.

Please use the links on the right side of this page to learn more about our internship program.

Our program is fully accredited by the American Psychological Association and we are members of APPIC. Our program participates in the APPIC Match (Program Code Number 156811) and follows all APPIC Match policies. This internship site agrees to abide by the APPIC policy that no person at this training facility will solicit, accept, or use any ranking-related information from any intern applicant.
