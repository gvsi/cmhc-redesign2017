CMHC Summary of Charges

CMHC Charges:

  • Individual Counseling appointment - $10 (Effective fall semester 2015, CMHC individual counseling appointments increased from $5 to $10)
  • Psychiatric appointment - $15 (Effective fall semester 2015, CMHC psychiatic counseling appointments increased from $5 to $15)
  • No show or cancellation less than 24 hours for any of the above - $25
  • No show less than 24 hours for groups - $25

Free CMHC Services:

  • Brief Assessment and Treatment Recommendations
  • Groups and Classes
  • Workshops
  • Mind Body Lab
  • CMHC Crisis Line 512-471-2255(CALL)
Students can pay their bill on-line with a credit card at the "What I Owe" page. If you have questions, contact 512-475-7065. What I Owe Page

Avoiding Charges on What I Owe Page

For individuals who prefer to avoid having charges on their "What I Owe" Page, you will want to visit the UHS Cashier's Office on the 2nd floor. This will need to be done immediately following your appointment, or at the very least the same business day. At the end of the business day all the transactions are purged to the accounting office and submitted for inclusion to the what I owe page. Thus, the window to prevent that is only the same day of the appointment. UHS accepts, cash, check or credit card for payment.
