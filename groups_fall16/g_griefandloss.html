Grief & Loss Support Group

This group is closed for this semester, check back for the new schedule.

All groups are free and confidential.

Has someone important to you passed away either recently or in the past? Are you having trouble dealing with this loss and/or functioning on a daily basis? Do you often feel alone or isolated because of your loss? Do you wish you had people in your life to talk to about your experiences with grief? If so, please consider the Grief and Loss Support Group.

In this group, you will:

  • Learn about the grief process
  • Relate to others who are also dealing with grief
  • Work on expressing and accepting your emotions around grief
  • Be in a supportive environment to explore your relationship with your loved one who has passed
  • Address issues around coping with grief

In most cases, before joining a group or class, students meet with the group leaders to determine if a particular group is a good fit for them. To set up a pre-group interview, please call (512) 471-3515.
