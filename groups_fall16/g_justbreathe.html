Just Breathe: An Anxiety Support Group

This group is closed for this semester, check back for the new schedule.

All groups are free and confidential.

Do you worry a lot?
Do you find yourself struggling with racing thoughts?
Do you experience physiological sensations connected to anxiety?
Does your anxiety get in the way of your academic or social goals?

This group helps students connect with others who deal with anxiety, process how that anxiety affects their daily lives as well as learn skills and techniques to help manage anxious symptoms.

Before joining the group, interested students must attend an Information Session on Tuesday 10/4, from 2-3:30pm, to meet the facilitators and determine if this group would be a good fit for you. To sign up for the Information Session, please call (512) 471-3515.
