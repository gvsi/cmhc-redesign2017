Queer Voices

Wednesdays
11:30am - 1pm
Meets at the Gender & Sexuality Center, SAC 2.112

All groups are free and confidential.

This discussion group is designed to provide a welcoming and confidential space to talk about issues that queer folks face, including (but not limited to): family, friends, academics, sexual and gender identity, relationships, coming out, careers, stress, adjustment, identity, self-esteem, spirituality, or any other relevant topic. The goal of the group is to create an open and respectful space where queer folks can explore topics that impact our daily lives with support from our peers. The group will be led by Joey Hannah from UTâ€™s Counseling and Mental Health Center. If you are interested in participating, please come check out one of the group meetings, or feel free to contact Joey or the GSC staff with any questions. Students are not required to be CMHC clients to attend and will not have to go through any pre-group screening to join.
