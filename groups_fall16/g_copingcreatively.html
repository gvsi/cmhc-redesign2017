Coping with Life Creatively

This group is closed for this semester, check back for the new schedule.

All groups are free and confidential.

If you are interested in creating art, playing songs, and taking pictures of nature as ways to express your emotions and as relaxation tools, then this is the place for you. You will learn to cope with anxiety and depression in a creative way. No artistic experience required.

In most cases, before joining a group or class, students meet with the group leaders to determine if a particular group is a good fit for them. To set up a pre-group interview, please call (512) 471-3515.
