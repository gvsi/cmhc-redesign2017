Mindfulness Meditation Lunch Break

This group is closed for this semester, check back for the new schedule.

All groups are free and confidential.

This group is an opportunity for practicing mindful meditation with others. The group meets bi-weekly and is drop-in style, open to UT students, faculty and staff. No meditation experience is necessary, and brief instruction will be provided at the beginning of each group session. Meditation will be in chairs. Participants are welcome to bring cushions, mats, blankets, or bolsters.
