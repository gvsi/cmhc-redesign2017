Optimizing Your Potential: Mind-Body Connection (OYP)

This group is closed for this semester, check back for the new schedule.

All groups are free and confidential.

Optimizing Your Potential (OYP) is an interactive, experiential class designed to help students who want to learn more effective coping strategies for stress, anxiety, perfectionism, overwhelming emotions, physical health problems exacerbated by stress, and life in general. In this 8-week class, mindfulness-based concepts and practices will be introduced, which can help participants change their automatic (and sometimes self-destructive) ways of thinking and dealing with the stressors of life. Mindfulness is the practice of paying attention in the present moment, on purpose, and in an open, nonjudgmental way.

The class is structured, interactive, and skills-based. Participants will be asked to spend time outside of class practicing the skills being covered.

Prospective participants are asked to attend an Information Session on September 22nd at 1:15 p.m. before the class begins, so that the facilitators can provide more information and determine if this class would be a good fit. To register for the Information Session, please call CMHC at (512) 471-3515. The class itself will run from 9/29 through 11/17.
