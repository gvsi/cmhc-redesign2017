Build Your Social Confidence Group

This group is closed for this semester, check back next semester for the new schedule.

Do you have difficulty speaking up in groups? Is it hard for you to initiate conversations? Do you worry about how others are evaluating you and find yourself more "in your head" than in the conversation?

If so, consider this group, in which you will:

  • Meet with other students who are facing similar challenges
  • Identify and change thoughts associated with your feared situations
  • Practice facing your social fears in a safe and supportive environment
  • Build communication and social skills to help you increase your comfort and self-confidence in social or performance situations

If you are interested in participating in this group, please call (512) 471-3515. to register to attend the Information Session on June 2nd, 2-3pm. Participants must attend the Information Session to determine if this group is a good fit for them.
