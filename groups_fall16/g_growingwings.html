Growing Wings from Wounds: A Trauma Support Group

This group is closed for this semester, check back for the new schedule.

All groups are free and confidential.

Growing Wings from Wounds is a group designed for those who have experienced traumatic events in their lives or who come from backgrounds in which they did not feel safe. The healing journey following experiences of trauma can, at times, feel overwhelming and isolating. This group is designed to offer support, facilitate healing, and create a safe space to explore the impact of these experiences. We will explore trauma reactions, coping strategies, creating safety in relationships, and integrating individual experiences into our identities.

Trauma can take many forms. One of the primary focuses of this group will be on experiences of trauma related to oppression including racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, ableism, classism, etc.

In most cases, before joining a group or class, students meet with the group leaders to determine if a particular group is a good fit for them. To set up a pre-group interview, please call (512) 471-3515.
