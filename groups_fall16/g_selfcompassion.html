Self-Compassion and Well-Being

This group is closed for this semester, check back in the Fall for the new schedule.

All groups are free and confidential.

Are you thinking about doing something for your health but having trouble? Want to exercise more? Eat more fruits and vegetables? Or do something your doctor has recommended but can’t quite seem to do it? Then this class is for you!

Recent findings in Health Psychology suggest that self-compassion may be an untapped factor in getting ourselves to live healthier lives. In this class, we will explore the concept of self-compassion and attempt to apply it to a behavior we are attempting to change. Come and check out this 3 session class – the only prerequisite is that you have to want to change something!

Please attend an Information Session before the class begins so you and the facilitator can determine if the class is right for you. To register for the Information Session, call CMHC at (512) 471-3515
