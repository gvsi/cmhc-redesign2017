Resources

The Counseling and Mental Health Center (CMHC), located in the Student Services Building on the 5th floor, is open Monday-Friday 8-5 for regular appointments and urgent walk-in appointments for students in crisis. Students can walk in or call to make an appointment. Students can also call the free CMHC Crisis Line any time of day for support. If you’re worried about a student, and they’re unwilling to seek help, you can call the Behavior Concerns Advice Line 512-232-5050 to seek consultation on what else you can do to help.

There are a variety of campus and community resources that can support students who have thoughts of suicide. See below for some additional resources.

Campus Resources

    (512) 471-3515 (To make an appointment and talk with a member of the Brief Assessment Referral Team)
