Mission:

CMHC is committed to serving UT's diverse campus community by providing counseling, psychiatric, consultation, and prevention services that facilitate students' academic and life goals and enhance their personal growth and well-being.

Values:

  • Safety:
    Promoting the physical and emotional well-being of UT students and the campus community; providing an open and accepting environment in which students can explore their concerns.
  • Respect:
    Fostering a culture of inclusiveness that honors the differences among individuals.
  • Compassion:
    Experiencing genuine care and concern for the unique struggles faced by students.
  • Learning:
    Encouraging the development of awareness, knowledge, and skills as a lifelong process.
  • Empowerment:
    Offering students information, context, and support to enable them to optimally navigate their life circumstances.
  • Excellence:
    Upholding the highest professional and ethical standards and providing national leadership in the field of mental health in higher education.
  • Community:
    Promoting meaningful connection and collaboration, and continually striving to engage honestly and authentically in relationships.
