At times it may be helpful for a counselor to meet with a class, residence hall, or student group following a traumatic event such as a student death, death of a professor, or any distressing situation that affects a number of students. When responding to traumatic events, we typically:

  • Provide information about common reactions
  • Discuss coping strategies
  • Link students to appropriate services

To discuss how CMHC may be of assistance following a traumatic event, contact a CMHC administrator at 512-475-6990, or after hours contact the CMHC Crisis Line at 512-471-CALL (2255) (UT Students Only).
